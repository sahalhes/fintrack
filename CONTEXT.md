# CONTEXT

## Terms

- Personal Finance Tracker: the primary transaction dashboard at `/`.
- Balance Sheet: the existing accounts-and-balances feature set, kept available on a separate route.
- Transaction: a logged income or expense with amount, category, type, date, and optional note.
- Income: money coming in; stored as a positive amount and counted as positive in summaries.
- Expense: money going out; stored as a positive amount and counted as negative in summaries.
- Category: a transaction grouping, usually chosen from the preset list with a custom fallback when needed.
